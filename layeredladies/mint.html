Get Ready to Mint Your Layered Ladies

To mint your own Layered Lady NFTs, you'll first need to set up a crypto wallet like MetaMask. . This will allow you to securely purchase and hold your NFTs. Learn more about Metamask and alternatives on CoinGecko.
This collection is minted on the Polygon blockchain, so you'll need some MATIC tokens in your wallet to complete transactions. Polygon offers fast and low cost transactions compared to Ethereum mainnet. Read more about the benefits of Polygon here. Once you have a wallet set up and funded with MATIC, connect it to our website to start minting! Claim your own unique Layered Lady NFTs to join the community and unlock special benefits. Owning crypto collectibles is exciting, but does come with responsibility. Be sure to keep your secret recovery phrase and password safe when setting up your wallet. This will allow you to access your NFTs from anywhere. With your wallet ready, you'll be set to securely obtain your very own Layered Lady NFTs on Polygon!
