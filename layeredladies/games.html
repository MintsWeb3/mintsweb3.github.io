The potential for play

The Layered Lady NFTs aren't just unique digital art - they can also be used as playable game pieces! Since each one is a fully functional playing card, the opportunities for games are endless.
To start, we're excited to launch a version of 21 featuring Layered Lady playing collectibles. The beautiful artwork makes for a one-of-a-kind gaming experience. Try to get as close to 21 as possible in this classic game of skill and strategy.
In the future, we plan to rollout more gameplay options to engage the community. Our interactive NFTs are the perfect vehicle for everything from poker to roleplaying games and more. We can't wait to develop new ways for you to enjoy both the art and utility of Layered Ladies.
Playing games together will help build the project's interactive ecosystem. And since every NFT is unique, you get to game with a custom deck like no other. Be sure to check back for more releases as we expand the possibilities of NFTs as game pieces.
